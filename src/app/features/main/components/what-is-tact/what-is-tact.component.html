What is Tact?

Tact and FunC

FunC is a thin and direct wrapper around Fift that allows precise control over execution code. Tact offers a similarly direct execution model and brings powerful abstractions on top of it to improve clarity and safety of complex smart contracts.

Rich types

Familiar syntax and strong types compatible with TL-B. Arithmetic is always safe because integers have precise bounds. Tact helps you to perform necessary checks without silent failures or unexpected truncation.

Actor-oriented

Strongly-typed messages and well-organized handlers.

Gas safety

Tact makes writing and auditing multi-contract systems easier by offering first-class gas commitments and types. Compiler knows cost of execution of each portion of the code.
